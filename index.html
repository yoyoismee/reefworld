For NFT Collectors

Digital NFTs (Not a Fucking Token)

You can purchase NFTs with a total peace of mind since you can rest assured that our NFTs will never be a token, will not have any other benefit, and might not be on any public chain cuz otherwise the SEC will be all over the place.

Simple Payment

We try to come up with a simple payment method for you to buy NFTs - but please keep in mind that we can’t provide you an easy method to transfer or sell it (yeah because of SEC and, for this case, anti-money laundering issue).
